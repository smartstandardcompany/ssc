<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user initially requested a data entry application to track sales, expenses, and supplier payments. Over the course of development, this has evolved into a comprehensive business management ERP system named SSC Track.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Financial Management:** Sales (Bank/Cash/Online/Credit), Expenses (with categories/sub-categories), Supplier Payments, Customer Credit Management, Invoicing with an item master list.
- **HR Management:** Employee database, detailed salary payments (including bonus, overtime, advance, deductions), leave tracking (annual, sick), loan management, and an Employee Self-Service Portal for leave requests and payment acknowledgments.
- **Asset & Liability Tracking:** Document management with expiry alerts and file attachments, tracking of company fines/penalties, company loans, and partner investments/salaries.
- **Cash Flow Management:** Branch-to-branch cash transfers, a central company balance, and tracking of inter-branch dues with payback options.
- **Bank Reconciliation:** A powerful Bank Statement Analyzer that parses PDF/XLS files from Alinma and Albilad banks, automatically categorizes transactions (POS Sales, Fees, VAT, SADAD bills, SARIE transfers), reconciles POS sales against app data, and groups transactions by sender/receiver and POS machine.
- **Reporting & Analytics:** A multi-tab dashboard with KPIs (% of sales, vs previous period), charts, and alerts (branch loss, upcoming expenses, dues). An advanced reporting module with period-over-period and branch-to-branch comparisons. PDF/XLS export capabilities on all pages.
- **Administration:** Granular role-based access control, self-service settings for Email (SMTP) and WhatsApp (Twilio), company branding, data import from XLS, and a one-click database backup feature.
- **Deployment & Accessibility:** The application is a Progressive Web App (PWA) and has been prepared for deployment on Railway with a multi-stage Dockerfile.

**User's preferred language**: English
