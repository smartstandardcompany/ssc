<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user initially requested a data entry application to track sales, expenses, and supplier payments. Over the course of development, this has evolved into a comprehensive business management ERP system named SSC Track.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Financial Management:** Sales (Bank/Cash/Online/Credit), Expenses (with categories/sub-categories), Supplier Payments, Customer Credit Management, Invoicing with an item master list, Item-level Profit & Loss (P&L) reporting.
- **HR Management:** Employee database with Job Titles (affecting salary and permissions), detailed salary payments (including bonus, overtime, advance, deductions), leave tracking (annual, sick), loan management, an Employee Self-Service Portal, and Staff Shift Scheduling.
- **Stock Management:** Inventory tracking with item costs, stock-in from supplier invoices (with OCR for data entry), and stock-out tracking for usage (e.g., by a chef).
- **Asset & Liability Tracking:** Document management with expiry alerts and file attachments, tracking of company fines/penalties, company loans, and partner investments/salaries.
- **Cash Flow Management:** Branch-to-branch cash transfers, a central company balance, and tracking of inter-branch dues with payback options.
- **Bank Reconciliation:** A powerful Bank Statement Analyzer that parses PDF/XLS files, automatically categorizes transactions, and includes a reconciliation tool to compare bank POS deposits against app sales data.
- **Reporting & Analytics:** A multi-tab dashboard with KPIs, charts, and alerts. An advanced reporting module with period-over-period and branch-to-branch comparisons.
- **Administration:** Granular role-based access control, self-service settings for Email (SMTP) and WhatsApp (Twilio), company branding, data import from XLS, a one-click database backup feature, and an in-app deployment guide.
- **Deployment & Accessibility:** The application is a Progressive Web App (PWA) and is prepared for deployment on servers like GoDaddy, with instructions provided within the app.
