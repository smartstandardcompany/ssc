The user wants a data entry application to track sales, expenses, and supplier payments.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Sales Tracking:**

    - Log sales per branch with date.
    - Branches should be selectable from a list.

    - Track payments to suppliers (cash, bank, or credit).

    - Add categories for suppliers.
- **Expense Tracking:**
    - Log general expenses like salary, rent, maintenance, VAT, and insurance.
    - Add expenses by category.
- **Customer Management:**
    - Add customers and assign them to specific branches, noting some customers may be shared across all branches.
- **Reporting:**
    - Export data to PDF and XLS formats.
    - A credit sales report showing total credit given, received, and remaining.
    - Option to receive/update payments directly from the credit report.
    - Supplier report.
    - Category report.
- **User Management:**
    - User roles and access control.
- **Notifications:**
    - WhatsApp notifications for daily sales updates (total sales, total expenses per branch).
